GEO BASKET SURFERS

Geo Basket Surfers is an Android game where you must find cultural related stickers hidden throughout UMa.

To add the token to your collection, you must collect its AR digital token and win it by finishing the Basket Surfer challenge, where you must dodge obstacles and collect mini-tokens, as the scenario and player change depending on the token unlocked.
